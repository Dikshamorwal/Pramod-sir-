Biography

About Me

Dr. Pramod Kumar is a biomedical scientist currently serving as Scientist D at the ICMR-National Institute of Cancer Prevention and Research (NICPR), Noida, and as Assistant Professor at the Academy of Scientific and Innovative Research (AcSIR). With over a decade of research experience, his work sits at the intersection of microbiology, genomics, cancer biology, and molecular diagnostics.

His laboratory focuses on understanding the complex interplay between the human microbiome and cancer development, particularly in oral squamous cell carcinoma. By integrating metagenomic sequencing, transcriptomic profiling, and advanced molecular tools, his team aims to identify microbial biomarkers for early detection and develop CRISPR-based diagnostic platforms for point-of-care applications.

Dr. Kumar has published over 85 peer-reviewed articles in high-impact journals including Nature Communications, Genome Medicine, and Nature Biotechnology. His work has been cited more than 2,400 times, reflecting the global relevance of his contributions to biomedical science. He serves on the editorial boards of several international journals and regularly reviews for funding agencies including DBT, DST, and ICMR.

Beyond the laboratory, Dr. Kumar is deeply committed to mentorship and science education. He has guided 6 PhD students, 12 master's students, and several research fellows. He actively advocates for open science, reproducible research practices, and capacity building in molecular diagnostics across India.
