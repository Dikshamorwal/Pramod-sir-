Deciphering Oral Microbiome Signatures in Oral Cancer

₹48.5 Lakhs DST-SERB

Role: Principal Investigator

Multi-omics investigation of oral microbial dysbiosis in oral squamous cell carcinoma progression. Integrating metagenomics, transcriptomics, and metabolomics to identify microbial biomarkers for early detection and therapeutic targeting.

2024 – 2027
Active

Genomic Surveillance of Antimicrobial Resistance in Clinical Pathogens

₹65.2 Lakhs DBT

Role: Principal Investigator

Whole-genome sequencing and bioinformatic surveillance of antimicrobial resistance determinants in ESKAPE pathogens across tertiary care hospitals. Developing a real-time genomic epidemiology dashboard for regional AMR monitoring.

2021 – 2024
Completed

Immune Checkpoint Blockade in Head and Neck Squamous Cell Carcinoma

₹32.8 Lakhs ICMR

Role: Co-Principal Investigator

Comprehensive characterization of PD-L1 expression, tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte profiles, and immune checkpoint inhibitor response biomarkers in HPV-negative HNSCC patients. Clinical cohort study with multi-parametric immune profiling.

2024 – 2027
Active

CRISPR-based Diagnostics for Emerging Pathogens

₹55.0 Lakhs BIRAC

Role: Principal Investigator

Engineering CRISPR-Cas12a and Cas13a systems for rapid, multiplexed, point-of-care detection of emerging viral and bacterial pathogens. Developing paper-based lateral flow assays and portable fluorescence readout platforms for resource-limited settings.

2025 – 2029
Active

Spatial Transcriptomics of the Tumor Microenvironment in Oral Cancer

₹120 Lakhs Wellcome Trust / DBT India Alliance

Role: Principal Investigator

High-resolution spatial transcriptomic profiling of oral squamous cell carcinoma tissues using 10x Visium and Xenium platforms. Mapping cellular ecosystems, ligand-receptor interactions, and immune exclusion niches to identify novel therapeutic targets and prognostic signatures.

2020 – 2023
Completed

Gut Microbiome Signatures in Colorectal Cancer Progression

₹28.5 Lakhs SERB

Role: Principal Investigator

Case-control metagenomic study identifying gut microbial signatures as non-invasive biomarkers for early detection of colorectal cancer. Correlated microbial taxonomic and functional shifts with clinical staging, histopathological grading, and patient outcomes.
