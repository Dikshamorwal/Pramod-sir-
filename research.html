Scientific Approach

Our Research Philosophy

We believe that breakthroughs emerge at the convergence of disciplines — where microbial ecology meets cancer biology, where genome engineering meets point-of-care diagnostics, and where computational modelling meets clinical translation.

Integrative Multi-Omics

Combining metagenomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics to build comprehensive molecular portraits of disease.

Translational Pipeline

Moving from bench to bedside through biomarker validation, assay development, and clinical trial partnerships.

Open Science

Commitment to reproducible research, public data deposition, and freely available computational tools and pipelines.

Infrastructure

Core Facilities

Next-Generation Sequencing

Illumina NovaSeq 6000, MiSeq, and Oxford Nanopore MinION for whole-genome, metagenomic, and transcriptomic sequencing.

Molecular Biology Core

Quantitative PCR, digital droplet PCR, CRISPR-based assay development, and protein expression and purification facilities.

Computational Cluster

High-performance computing cluster with 256 cores, GPU nodes, and 100 TB storage for bioinformatics and machine learning.
