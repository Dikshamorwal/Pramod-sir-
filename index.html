Research Focus

Research Highlights

Exploring the molecular underpinnings of disease through interdisciplinary biomedical research.

Oral Microbiome & Cancer

Investigating the role of oral microbial dysbiosis in oral squamous cell carcinoma progression. Identifying microbial biomarkers for early detection and therapeutic targeting using metagenomic sequencing.

Learn more

Cancer Genomics & Biomarkers

Characterizing genomic alterations and transcriptomic signatures in head and neck cancers. Developing liquid biopsy approaches for non-invasive cancer detection and monitoring therapeutic response.

Learn more

CRISPR-Based Molecular Diagnostics

Engineering CRISPR-Cas systems for rapid, point-of-care detection of infectious diseases and cancer-associated mutations. Developing portable diagnostic platforms for resource-limited settings.
